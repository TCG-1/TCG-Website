# Service Detail Pages — Collective Sub-Page Audit

All 7 service detail pages follow the same template at `/services/[slug]`.

---

## Common Template Structure

Each page contains:
1. JsonLd: WebPage + Breadcrumb + Service schema
2. Overview section with H1, overview paragraph (~40 words), 2 CTAs
3. Two-column layout: "When to use this" (3 bullets) + "What we deliver" (3 bullets)
4. Global CTA banner

**Total unique content per page:** ~120–160 words (critically thin)

## Common SEO Issues Across All 7 Pages

1. **Critical: Thin content** — ~120–160 words per page. Google expects substantially more for commercial service pages. Risk of being treated as doorway pages or thin content.
2. **No testimonials or social proof** — no stats, quotes, or case references
3. **No images** — pages are text-only apart from the CTA banner
4. **No FAQ schema** — missed opportunity for SERP features
5. **No related blog posts** — no internal linking to supporting content
6. **No industry cross-references** — no mention of which industries each service applies to
7. **No pricing signals** — not even "Get a quote" or "Packages start from…"
8. **All pages have the same "When to use" / "What we deliver" pattern** — no content differentiation between pages beyond bullet text

## Internal Linking Observations

- Each page links to: `/discovery-call`, `/on-site-assessment`
- Breadcrumb links to: Home → Services (operational-excellence-consulting-uk) → Current page
- **Missing links to:** other service detail pages, industry pages, blog posts, mentoring page

---

## What Should Be Improved (All 7 Pages)

1. Expand content to 800–1,200 words minimum per page with methodology explanation, outcomes, and related industry applications
2. Add FAQ section with 3–5 service-specific questions and schema markup
3. Add related industry links (e.g. cost management → manufacturing, energy)
4. Add testimonials relevant to each service area
5. Add related blog post links
6. Add images specific to each service
7. Cross-link between complementary service pages
