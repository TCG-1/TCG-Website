---

## Common Template Structure

Each page contains:
1. JsonLd: WebPage + Breadcrumb (no Service schema — missed opportunity)
2. Overview section with H1, overview paragraph (~40 words), 2 CTAs
3. Two-column layout: "Sector challenges" (3 bullets) + "Why Tacklers" (3 bullets)
4. Global CTA banner

**Total unique content per page:** ~120–150 words (critically thin)

## Common SEO Issues Across All 6 Pages

1. **Critical: Thin content** — same issue as service pages. ~120–150 words per page.
2. **No Service schema** — industry pages should include Service schema with areaServed and serviceType
3. **No testimonials or case studies** — no industry-specific social proof
4. **No images** — text-only beyond the CTA banner
5. **No FAQ schema** — missed SERP opportunity
6. **No related services links** — no mention of which services apply to each industry
7. **No blog cross-links** — no supporting content linked
8. **Breadcrumb shows "Industry"** rather than the industry name as breadcrumb text

---

## What Should Be Improved (All 6 Pages)

1. Expand content to 800–1,200 words per page with industry-specific methodology, typical challenges, and outcomes
2. Add Service schema with industry-relevant serviceType
3. Add related service links (e.g. aerospace → cost management, supplier quality)
4. Add industry-specific testimonials or anonymised case references
5. Add FAQ section with industry-specific questions and schema
6. Add relevant images
7. Cross-link to related blog posts (when available)
8. Fix breadcrumb to display industry name: Home → Services → "Aerospace & Defence"
