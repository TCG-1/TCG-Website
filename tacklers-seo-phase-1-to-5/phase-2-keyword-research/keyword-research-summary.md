## Research Methodology

Keywords were researched across the following dimensions:
1. Core commercial intent keywords aligned to existing services
2. Supporting informational intent keywords for content authority
3. Long-tail problem-solution keywords reflecting buyer pain points
4. Industry-specific keyword clusters mapped to the six sectors served
5. Mentoring and training-specific keywords
6. On-site assessment and Gemba-specific keywords
7. Competitor keyword gap inference from UK consulting firms in the same niche

Volume estimates use UK monthly search volume ranges. Difficulty is estimated on a Low / Medium / High scale based on SERP competition density for UK results.
