## SEO Observations

**Strengths:**
- Title tag at 51 characters — within ideal range
- Meta description at 146 characters — good length
- Strong keyword coverage across 7 keywords
- Service schema present
- FAQ schema with 5 mentoring-specific questions
- "500+" stat adds credibility
- "30% average waste reduction" is a compelling stat

**Weaknesses:**
- H1 is 72 characters — long for an H1; could be tighter
- "Download brochure" CTA links to `/contact` — misleading; no brochure exists
- Programme cards all link to `/book-lean-training` with no distinct content per programme
- No individual programme detail pages exist
- No mention of programme duration, pricing signals, or certification
- No blog cross-links for Lean training topics

## What Is Missing

1. Individual programme detail pages or expanded sections for each programme
2. A real downloadable brochure or PDF
3. Trainer credentials section (Audrey's background)
4. Client testimonials specific to training
5. Programme structure details (duration, format, cohort size)
6. Cross-links to blog posts about Lean training

1. Shorten H1 to ~50 characters: "Practical Lean Training and Mentoring UK"
2. Replace "Download brochure" CTA with either a real download or "Contact us"
3. Add programme detail content (duration, format, outcomes) to each programme card or create sub-pages
4. Add trainer credentials section
5. Add 2–3 training-specific testimonials
6. Add internal links to relevant blog content and service pages
