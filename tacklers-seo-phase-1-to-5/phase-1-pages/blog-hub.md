## SEO Observations

**Strengths:**
- Title tag at 70 characters — slightly long but descriptive
- Strong intro copy with internal links to services, mentoring, and discovery call
- 5 keywords defined in metadata
- CollectionPage schema + WebPage + Breadcrumb structured data
- Revalidation set to 300 seconds for fresh content

**Weaknesses:**
- Only 4 blog posts — critically low volume for topical authority
- No category filtering, tags, or archive navigation
- No pagination (though not needed with 4 posts)
- No author archive or author pages
- No search functionality despite SearchAction in WebSite schema
- Blog posts have no internal cross-links within article body text

---

## Internal Linking Observations

- Links to: `/discovery-call`, `/on-site-assessment`, `/operational-excellence-consulting-uk`, `/lean-training-uk`, each blog post
- Missing links to: `/about`, `/careers`, service detail pages, industry pages
