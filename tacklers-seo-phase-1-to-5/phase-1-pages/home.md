---

## SEO Observations

**Strengths:**
- Title tag includes primary keyword "Lean Transformation" and brand
- H1 is unique and includes "People-First Lean Transformation"
- Comprehensive structured data: WebPage, Breadcrumb, FAQ
- Dual CTAs above the fold
- Social proof (stats, testimonials, Companies House link)
- Keyword density is natural and well-distributed
- FAQ schema present with 6 questions
- Canonical set correctly

**Weaknesses:**
- Title tag is 82 characters — slightly over the recommended 60-character display limit; may be truncated in SERPs
- Meta description is 162 characters — within acceptable range but could be tighter
- No dedicated keyword targeting for "gemba consulting" despite Gemba being a core differentiator
- Service cards link to `/operational-excellence-consulting-uk` (one URL) for 4 out of 6 items, concentrating link equity rather than distributing to service detail pages
- Blog section shows only 3 posts and all are generic — no topical relevance signals
- No schema.org `HowTo` or `ItemList` for the four-stage methodology

---

## Internal Linking Observations

- Links to: `/discovery-call`, `/on-site-assessment`, `/operational-excellence-consulting-uk`, `/lean-training-uk`, `/about`, `/blog`, 6 industry pages
- Missing links to: individual service detail pages from homepage service cards, `/careers`, `/support`, `/case-studies` (does not exist yet)
- Footer links cover all core navigation plus legal pages

---

## UX Observations

- Hero slideshow provides visual variety
- Clear visual hierarchy with sections alternating white/slate/dark backgrounds
- Stats carousel adds credibility but could be more specific (e.g. "£2.4M+ savings" rather than "£M+")
- Founder section is strong but positioned late on the page
- Mobile responsiveness appears well-handled through Tailwind responsive classes

---

## Content Observations

- Copy is well-written, confident, and avoids generic filler
- "We reduce waste while protecting and redeploying your talent" is used as a unifying brand line — effective differentiation
- Some service card descriptions are vague ("That is not a philosophy—it is simply reality") — could be more keyword-rich
- Industries section uses short descriptions that lack keyword density

---

## Metadata Observations

- OG image set to hero slideshow image — good
- OG type: website — correct
- Twitter card: summary_large_image — correct
- Locale: en_GB — correct
- Google verification tag present

---

## What Is Missing

1. Direct links from homepage service cards to their dedicated `/services/[slug]` detail pages
2. A "Results" or "Case Studies" section with concrete outcomes
3. Schema markup for the four-stage methodology (HowTo or ItemList)
4. A newsletter signup CTA on the homepage
5. An explicit mention of UK geographic coverage for local SEO

---

## What Should Be Improved

1. Shorten title tag to ≤60 characters: "Lean Transformation & Operational Excellence UK | Tacklers"
2. Link each service card to its dedicated service detail page instead of the services hub
3. Add industry-specific keywords to industry card descriptions
4. Add a "Results" highlight section (even 2–3 bullet metrics) near the top of the page
5. Consider moving the founder credibility section higher to support E-E-A-T
6. Add a newsletter signup component to capture email leads
