## SEO Observations

**Strengths:**
- Clear contact information (email, phone, hours)
- FAQ schema present
- WebPage + Breadcrumb structured data
- Lead capture form functional
- "We go where the work happens" reinforces brand positioning

**Weaknesses:**
- Title tag format uses pipes and is 54 characters — acceptable but "Contact" alone is generic
- No embedded map or address (firm has no fixed office, which is stated — this is fine but could be handled with service area markup)
- No LocalBusiness-specific contact schema on this page
- Operating hours shown in content but not repeated in page-level structured data
