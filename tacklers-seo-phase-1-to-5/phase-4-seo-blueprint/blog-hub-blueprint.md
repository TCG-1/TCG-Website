## Blueprint

### Title
**Proposed:** Lean Consulting Blog — Insights From the Gemba — Tacklers (60 chars)

### Meta description
**Proposed:** Practical insights on lean transformation, operational excellence, and continuous improvement from UK consultants in the field. (124 chars)

### Content additions

1. **Hub introduction** (150–200 words) — explain what the blog covers, who it's for, and how it connects to Tacklers' consulting practice. Include keywords: operational excellence, lean transformation, continuous improvement, Gemba.

2. **Category navigation** — add category filters or section headings:
   - Operational Excellence
   - Lean Methodology
   - Industry Insights
   - Leadership & Capability
   - Case Studies & Results

3. **Featured post section** — highlight the latest or most important post at the top

4. **Related content CTAs** — at bottom of hub:
   - "Explore our consulting services" → `/operational-excellence-consulting-uk`
   - "Book a discovery call" → `/discovery-call`

### Blog post template (for all future posts)
Every blog post must include:

1. **Word count:** Minimum 1,200 words (pillar posts: 2,000+)
2. **Structure:** H1 (title) → intro (80 words) → H2 sections → conclusion → CTA
3. **Internal links:** Minimum 3–5 per post:
   - 1 link to a service page
   - 1 link to an industry page (if relevant)
   - 1 link to a conversion page (discovery call / assessment)
   - 1–2 links to related posts
4. **Author attribution:** Author name, photo, 1-line bio, link to about page
5. **Schema:** BlogPosting with author, datePublished, dateModified
6. **Featured image:** Custom image with descriptive alt text
7. **Meta description:** Unique, ≤155 chars, includes primary keyword
8. **Categories:** Assign to at least one cluster category
9. **Related posts:** Show 2–3 related posts at bottom
