# Blog Content Blueprint — tacklersconsulting.com

---

## Blog Content Strategy

### Content pillars
1. **Operational Excellence** — what it is, frameworks, case applications
2. **Lean Methodology** — tools, techniques, practical guides (Gemba, VSM, Kaizen, Standard Work)
3. **Industry Insights** — sector-specific operational challenges and solutions
4. **Leadership & Capability** — building internal lean capability, leadership development
5. **Results & Lessons** — case study narratives, lessons learned, what worked

### Content types
| Type | Purpose | Target length | Frequency |
|------|---------|---------------|-----------|
| Pillar guide | Rank for head terms, anchor clusters | 2,000–3,000 words | 1/month |
| Problem-solution post | Convert mid-funnel searchers | 1,200–1,800 words | 2/month |
| Industry insight | Build sector authority | 1,200–1,500 words | 1/month |
| Tool/methodology guide | Capture informational intent | 1,500–2,000 words | 1/month |
| Case study narrative | Demonstrate results, build trust | 800–1,200 words | 1/month |

## Blog Post SEO Checklist (for every post)

### Before writing
- [ ] Primary keyword identified and not cannibalising existing content
- [ ] Target word count set
- [ ] Outline with H2/H3 headings created
- [ ] Internal link targets identified (min 3–5)

### During writing
- [ ] Primary keyword in title, H1, first 100 words
- [ ] Secondary keywords woven into H2 headings and body
- [ ] British English throughout
- [ ] Practical, actionable content — not promotional fluff
- [ ] Examples, data, or frameworks included
- [ ] No unsupported claims

### Before publishing
- [ ] Title tag ≤ 60 chars with primary keyword in first 4 words
- [ ] Meta description ≤ 155 chars with primary keyword and CTA
- [ ] Author name and bio attached
- [ ] Featured image with descriptive alt text
- [ ] 3–5 internal links verified
- [ ] CTA to discovery call or relevant conversion page
- [ ] Related posts section populated
- [ ] BlogPosting schema with author, datePublished, dateModified
- [ ] Category assigned
