## Content Depth Gap by Page Type

### Service Detail Pages
**Current:** ~140 words per page. Overview paragraph + 3–4 bullet points.
**Competitor benchmark:** 800–1,500 words with methodology sections, expected outcomes, typical engagement structure, relevant case study reference, FAQ.

**What's missing per service page:**
1. Detailed methodology description (how Tacklers delivers this service)
2. Typical engagement timeline and structure
3. Expected outcomes and metrics
4. Who this service is for (decision-maker persona)
5. Related case study or client example
6. FAQ section (2–3 questions)
7. Cross-links to related services
8. Cross-links to relevant industries

### Industry Pages
**Current:** ~130 words per page. Overview + 3–4 challenges + 3–4 "Why Tacklers" points.
**Competitor benchmark:** 800–1,200 words with sector context, specific challenges with data, relevant services mapped, compliance/regulatory context, case study reference.

**What's missing per industry page:**
1. Sector overview with UK market data or statistics
2. Specific operational challenges with detail (not just bullet headers)
3. Regulatory or compliance context (especially aerospace, healthcare, energy)
4. How Tacklers' methodology applies in this sector
5. Relevant services mapped with links
6. Case study reference or results data
7. Testimonial from sector client

### Blog Posts
**Current:** 330–420 words. No subheadings, no internal links, no visuals, no author attribution.
**Competitor benchmark:** 1,200–2,500 words with clear structure, H2/H3 subheadings, internal links, author bio, call to action, related posts.

**What's missing per blog post:**
1. Depth — at least 1,200 words for ranking potential
2. Structured subheadings (H2, H3)
3. Practical examples, data, or frameworks
4. Internal links (3–5 per post)
5. Author attribution with photo and bio
6. CTA to relevant service or conversion page
7. Related posts section

## Missing Content Types

### 1. Case Studies (CRITICAL)
**Why it matters:** Every competitor in the UK lean consulting space features case studies. They serve as:
- Primary E-E-A-T signal (Experience, Expertise)
- Commercial intent conversion tool
- Long-tail keyword targeting vehicle
- Social proof beyond testimonials

**Proposed structure per case study:**
- Client industry + size (anonymised if needed)
- Challenge / starting point
- Approach / methodology applied
- Results (quantified)
- Client quote (if available)
- Timeline

**Minimum viable:** 5 case studies covering 5 different industries.

### 2. Methodology Pillar Content
**Why it matters:** The site mentions Gemba, VSM, Kaizen, Standard Work, and Hoshin Kanri but has no dedicated content explaining these methodologies. This is a massive informational keyword gap.

**Proposed content:**
- "What Is Operational Excellence?" — pillar guide (2,000+ words)
- "What Is Gemba?" — pillar guide (1,500+ words)
- "Value Stream Mapping Guide" — practical guide (2,000+ words)
- "Continuous Improvement Framework" — pillar guide (1,500+ words)

### 3. Downloadable Resources
**Why it matters:** Lead capture opportunity and backlink attractant. Also referenced in mentoring page CTA.

**Proposed resources:**
- Gemba Walk Checklist (PDF)
- Lean Readiness Assessment Template (PDF)
- Value Stream Mapping Template (PDF)

### 4. FAQ Hub Page
**Why it matters:** The site has FAQs distributed across multiple pages but no dedicated FAQ page. A central FAQ hub can target People Also Ask boxes and long-tail queries.

### 5. Comparison / Decision Content
**Why it matters:** Mid-funnel content for buyers comparing approaches.

**Proposed content:**
- "Lean vs Six Sigma: Which Is Right for Your Organisation?"
- "When to Hire a Lean Consultant vs Building Internal Capability"
- "What to Look for in an Operational Excellence Consultant"

## Content Cannibalisation Issues

### High Risk: Three December 2024 Blog Posts
| Post | Primary topic |
|------|--------------|
| Enhance Operational Excellence with Tacklers Consulting Group | Operational excellence + Tacklers services |
| Drive Efficiency with Lean Methodologies at Tacklers Consulting | Lean efficiency + Tacklers services |
| Maximise Productivity with Tacklers Consulting Group Services | Productivity + Tacklers services |

**Problem:** All three posts are promotional pieces covering overlapping topics (lean + efficiency + operational improvement) published on the same date. Google will struggle to determine which to rank.

**Recommendation:**
- **Option A:** Consolidate into one comprehensive post titled "How Lean Methodologies Drive Operational Excellence" (1,500+ words)
- **Option B:** Rewrite each with distinct angles — e.g., one focused on methodology, one on industry application, one on results/metrics

### Medium Risk: Services Pillar vs Service Detail Pages
The services pillar page mentions all 11 service areas. If service detail pages are expanded but the pillar page also contains detailed service descriptions, cannibalisation could occur.

**Mitigation:** Keep the pillar page as a hub (overview + links) and move detailed content to individual service pages.
