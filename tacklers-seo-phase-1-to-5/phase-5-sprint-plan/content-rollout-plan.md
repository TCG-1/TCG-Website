## Content Quality Standards

### Every piece of content must:
1. Target a specific primary keyword (no keyword-less content)
2. Be written in British English
3. Contain unique, practical information (no fluff, no redundancy)
4. Include 3–5 internal links (see internal linking rollout plan)
5. Feature evidence or examples where claims are made
6. End with a clear CTA
7. Be reviewed against the blog SEO checklist before publishing

### Content that must NOT be published:
- Posts under 800 words
- Promotional-only content with no informational value
- Content that cannibalises an existing page's primary keyword
- Content without internal links
- Content with claims unsupported by data or case reference

---

## Content Production Workflow

1. **Brief:** Primary keyword, target word count, internal link targets, outline
2. **Draft:** Written in British English, following the template for its content type
3. **Review:** SEO checklist pass — keywords, links, metadata, schema
4. **Publish:** Deploy to site with all metadata and schema in place
5. **Verify:** Check in Google Search Console within 48 hours — ensure indexed
